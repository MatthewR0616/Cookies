COOKIES!


Cookies. I like them. You like them. Everyone likes them.

I like them so much, I'm going to teach you how to make them!

What you'll need:

  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick, 4 ounces) butter
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 cup (8 ounces) dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chopped toasted nuts (optional)
  • 2-21/2 semisweet chocolate chips

Once you have all of your ingredients, its time to start baking!

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375⚬F
  2. In a large mixing bowl, beat together the butter, shortening, sugars, vanilla and almond extracts, egg, salt and baking soda.
  3. Beat until the mixture is smooth and light in color.
  4. Add flour, nuts and chocolate chips, mixing at a slow speed untin thoroughly combined.
  5. Scoop mixture by tablespoons onto lightly greased or parchment lined cookie sheets, spacing then 11/2inches apart.
  6. Bake the cookies for 12 to 14 minutes
  7. To check for readiness, use a spatula and tilt a cookie to 45 degree angle. If the bottom stays intact, the cookies are ready
  8. Transfer cookies to a rack to cool
Once the cookies have cooled to your liking, enjoy with a big glass of milk!
